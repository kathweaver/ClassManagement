You are to work with your partner to write a story about Jose and his trusty horse, Froggie. Each partner is to write three lines of the story using your word processor. Save in in a file called SagaOfJose.doc

After you have written your three lines, paste the entire story into an email and email it to your partner. When you receive your email, copy and paste the story into your SagaOfJose.doc file. When you have finished your story, both partners will submit it using the class website.

As you write, keep the following in mind:

  • Even though Jose tries to be a good cowboy, he gets into a lot of trouble.
  • Jose has a beautiful true love names Rosie. She often has to get Jose out of some kind of trouble.
  • Fortunately, Jose’s horse, Froggie, is the fastest and smartest horse in the entire West. Froggie has saved Jose from disaster many times.
  • Jose lives on a ranch somewhere in the “Wild West”.
  • Jose’s favorite drink is Diet Big Red soda (otherwise known as Big Red Lite).
  • Jose always has a pocket full of peppermint candies to share with his friends and with Froggie.

Rules
1. Each person must write only three lines before sending it to their partner.

2. Before writing your three lines during each turn, type your initials in parentheses and then start to write.

EXAMPLE: Everyone’s story should start with these opening lines and continue with your own words: (RLH) The sun was going down as Jose rode into town on his trusty horse, Froggie. He was very tired and thirsty, but . . .

3. Keep the story clean and appropriate or it will not be graded.

4. Give your story a nice appearance by creating a cover page. Use clip art and word art to make your cover page look great.

5. This assignment should be fun! Create as many funny and interesting characters as you want and get them in and out of situations. Give it a happy ending.
