Presentation Project

You are going to create a Power Power Presentation. This presentation will allow you to present information about a country you have selected. You may select any country you wish. Probably the easiest country to pick would be the one you were born in. If you were born in the U.S.A., for example, you can do your presentation on the U.S.A. If you do not want to do the country you were born in, you can pick a country you are interested in.

You will need to have at least 10 slides in your presentation, but no more than 20. The first slide must be a title slide and the last slides must be at least credit slide. Any information you obtain from another source, either internet, or an reference book, must be included on the credit slide(s).

You must include at least the following:

  • Picture of the countries flag
  • Map showing where the country is located
  • Form of government
  • Primary Language(s) spoken

Other items you might include

  • Major monuments
  • Major points of interest - these can be cities or natural features (example, Grand Canyon)
  • Major holidays -- country holidays, like 4th of July -- not religious holidays unless they are unique to that country
  • Traditional clothing
  • Traditional foods
  • Traditional occupations -- especially if unique to that country
  • Major industries
